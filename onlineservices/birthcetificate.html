Download Birth and Death Certificate

1. Visit the Birth & Death Registration official website at Birth and Death website

2. Go to Certificate Download and then click Birth or Death which you want to download

3. Enter required details and then click view

4. After click view button. It will list out the persons who are all born on the mentioned date.

5. Click the registration number which you want to download. It will show the Certificate verify the details and then save the file.

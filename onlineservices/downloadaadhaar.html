Download Aadhaar Online - YouTube Video

To Download Aadhaar card , follow these steps:

1. Visit the UIDAI Official Website

Go to the official website of the Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI) at https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in/.

2. Login with Aadhaar

You will need to log in with your Aadhaar number. Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number and the security code. You will receive an OTP (One Time Password) on your registered mobile number.

4. OTP Verification

Enter the OTP you received on your registered mobile number to verify your identity.

5. Download Aadhaar

Once you are logged in, you can find the download aadhaar link under Services.

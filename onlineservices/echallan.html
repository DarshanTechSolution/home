eChallan Online Payment

1. Visit the Kalanjiyam website at eChallan Website

2. Enter the Remitter details

  • Remitter Name
  • Contact Number
  • Block/Street
  • City/Town/Village
  • PIN Code
  • Email ID for receiving of Challan Copy

3. Fill the Department details

  • District
  • Service Rendering department

DDO Code will automatically generated

Note: You should know the department name for payment

3. Fill the Service details

  • Recipt Type
  • Sub Type

    • Acct Code automatically generated

  • Amount

Note: Remarks are optional you may give for your reference.

5. Do the payment. after made online payment the challan will be generate, after that contact the concern department
